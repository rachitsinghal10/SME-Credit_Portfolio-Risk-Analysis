# Early Warning System (EWS) Risk Monitoring

## Project Overview

This project demonstrates how an Early Warning System (EWS) can be built to monitor borrower behaviour and identify risky loan accounts.

The model uses behavioural indicators such as payment delays, credit utilization, sales trends and cheque bounce frequency to classify borrowers into risk categories.

## Dataset Fields

Account_ID
Branch
Sector
Outstanding_Amount
EMI_Delay_Days
Credit_Utilization_%
Sales_Trend
Cheque_Bounce_Count

## Risk Indicators

Payment Delay Risk
Utilization Risk
Sales Risk
Bounce Risk

## Risk Scoring Model

Low Risk = 0
Medium Risk = 1
High Risk = 2

Total Score Range = 0–8

## Final Risk Classification

0–2 → Low Risk  
3–4 → Medium Risk  
5–8 → High Risk

To demonstrate credit risk monitoring techniques used in banking portfolios.
